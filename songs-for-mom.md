## Additional Resources by Song

### If This Is The Last Time - LANY
- **Simple Chords:** Use Capo 4 with G, D, Em, C (beginner-friendly open chords)
- **Alternative:** [ChordsEasy (simplified option)](https://chordseasy.com/song/35096/if-this-is-the-last-time/)

### The Middle - Jimmy Eat World
- **Key:** D Major
- **Chords:** Only 3 chords! D, G, A repeat throughout
- **Melody Analysis:** [Hooktheory](https://www.hooktheory.com/theorytab/view/jimmy-eat-world/the-middle)

### Sing Your Praise To The Lord - Amy Grant
- **Key:** Eb Major, 88 BPM
- **Sheet Music:** [Sheet Music Plus (paid)](https://www.sheetmusicplus.com/en/product/sing-your-praise-to-the-lord-19424199.html)
- **Easy Guitar:** [Sheet Music Now](https://www.sheetmusicnow.com/products/sing-your-praise-to-the-lord-p398261)

### Annie's Song - John Denver
- **Key:** D (or G to avoid barre chords)
- **Chord Progression:** 1 – 4 – 5 – 6m – 4 – 5
- **Tutorial:** [Heartwood Guitar](https://www.heartwoodguitar.com/chords/john-denver-annies-song/)

### Let It Be - The Beatles
- **Key:** C Major (no sharps or flats!)
- **Free PDF Options:**
  - [Full sheet music](https://sheetsfree.com/sheets/B/The%20Beatles%20-%20Let%20It%20Be.pdf)
  - [Easy Piano with Chords/Lyrics](https://sheetsfree.com/sheets/B/The%20Beatles%20-%20Let%20It%20Be%20(Easy%20Piano%20Chords%20&%20Lyrics).pdf)
- **Video Tutorial:** [Pianote](https://www.pianote.com/blog/how-to-play-let-it-be-piano/)

### Take Me Home, Country Roads - John Denver
- **Key:** G (Capo 2) or A (no capo)
- **Chords:** G, C, D, Em (with one F in bridge - use easy version)
- **Video Lesson:** [Song Notes](https://songnotes.net/lessons/151/)

### Somewhere Out There - An American Tail
- **Key:** C Major
- **Chords:** C, C/E, F, G, Dm7, Em, Am
- **Free Piano Sheet:** [EveryonePiano](https://www.everyonepiano.com/Music-8368-Somewhere-Out-There-The-Main-Theme-of-An-American-Tail.html)

### Awesome God - Rich Mullins
- **Key:** Em, 74 BPM
- **Simplified Chords:** [PraiseCharts (multiple keys)](https://www.praisecharts.com/songs/details/38349/awesome-god-simplified-chords)
- **Free PDF:** [LHUMC Chord Chart](https://www.lhumc.org/hp_wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/Awesome-God-Chords-Em.pdf)

2. **Let It Be** - Key of C, very common chord progression
3. **Annie's Song** - Beautiful waltz, simple chord shapes
4. **Awesome God** - Em-based, easy worship song
